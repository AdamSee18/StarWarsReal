Episode 1

The Phantom Menace is set 32 years before the events of the original Star Wars movie. It follows Jedi Knight Qui-Gon Jinn and his apprentice, Obi-Wan Kenobi, as they try to protect Queen Amidala from the evil Trade Federation. Along the way, they meet a young boy named Anakin Skywalker, who has a strong connection to the Force. The film also introduces important characters such as the Sith Lord Darth Maul and the Gungan warrior Jar Jar Binks
